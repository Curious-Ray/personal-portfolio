Refund Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Short version: Free users have nothing to refund. Paid users get 7 days, no questions asked.

1. Free users (current)

The Extension is currently free for early users. There is nothing to refund. If it doesn't work for you, just uninstall it from Chrome.

2. Paid users (once paid pricing begins)

Once paid pricing turns on (US$15 lifetime), every new purchase includes a 7-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If you're not happy with the Extension for any reason within 7 days of purchase, you'll get a full refund.

3. How to request a refund

Email thapadeepak726@gmail.com from the email address you used to purchase. Include:

  • your order or transaction ID (from your Dodo Payments receipt);
  • a brief reason (optional — "I just want a refund" works).

We'll process the refund through Dodo Payments within 3 business days. The actual time it takes to appear on your statement depends on your bank or card issuer (usually 5–10 business days).

4. Exceptions

After 7 days, refunds are at our discretion. We're indie and fair — if you have an exceptional situation, email us and we'll work something out. We reserve the right to refuse refund requests that appear to be abuse (e.g., serial buy-and-refund patterns).

5. Chargebacks

Please email us first instead of filing a chargeback. Chargebacks are slow, expensive for both sides, and a polite email almost always gets you a faster refund.
