// HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. That's the whole product.

STEP 1

Install & open Magnific.

Add the extension to Chrome, log into your Magnific account, and open either the image or video generator.

STEP 2

Paste your prompt list.

One prompt per line. The extension reads the list, then configures each run with your chosen resolution, concurrency, and delay.

STEP 3

Press start. Walk away.

The queue runs in order, waits for each render to finish, and starts the next one — even if you switch tabs. Come back to a finished library.

// FAQ

Questions

Is Magnific Automation made by Magnific?

No. Magnific Automation is an independent third-party Chrome extension, not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Magnific. It's a browser tool that automates actions you'd otherwise do manually on Magnific using your own account.

Do I need a Magnific account?

Yes — you use your own Magnific account and your own credits. The extension doesn't include Magnific access; it just automates how you interact with the site once you're logged in.

Is it really free?

Yes — 100% free for early users. No credit card, no trial that expires, no upsell. You install from the Chrome Web Store and use the full product. The only catch: once I have enough users to consider the product battle-tested, the price flips to $15 lifetime for new customers. Everyone who installed during the free launch stays free forever.

Why is it free right now?

I'm early. I need real users running real batches with this to find rough edges faster than I ever could alone. Your feedback during launch is worth more than $15 to me. Once it's rock-solid and there's a user base, the price goes up for new customers — but you'll be grandfathered in at free for life.

Is it one-time or a subscription?

Neither currently — it's free. When paid pricing turns on, it will be a one-time $15 lifetime license. Never a subscription, never auto-renew, never a per-generation charge.

What if Magnific changes their UI and the extension breaks?

When Magnific rolls out meaningful UI changes, the extension gets patched and pushed to the Chrome Web Store. Updates are free for the lifetime of your license.

What's the refund policy?

Free users: nothing to refund — just uninstall from Chrome if it doesn't work for you. Once paid pricing kicks in ($15 lifetime), all new purchases will include a 7-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. Email thapadeepak726@gmail.com and I'll process the refund via Dodo Payments.

Is my Magnific password safe?

The extension never sees, stores, or transmits your Magnific password. It operates inside your already-logged-in browser session, the same way any user action on the page would. No credentials are sent anywhere.

What browsers are supported?

Google Chrome and any Chromium-based browser that supports Chrome Web Store extensions (Brave, Edge, Arc, Opera). Firefox and Safari are not currently supported.
