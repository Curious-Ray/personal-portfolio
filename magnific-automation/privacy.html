Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

1. Who we are

Magnific Automation (the "Extension") is operated by Deepak Thapa, an independent developer based in Kathmandu, Nepal. Contact: thapadeepak726@gmail.com.

2. What we don't collect

The Extension is privacy-first by design. It does not:

  • read, store, or transmit your Magnific password or any other credentials;
  • read or transmit any data from web pages outside Magnific;
  • contain analytics or third-party tracking code;
  • create accounts on our servers.

The Extension runs entirely inside your own Chrome browser session. Your prompts and generated content stay between you and Magnific — they never touch our infrastructure.

3. What may be collected (when paid pricing turns on)

When you purchase a paid license (US$15 lifetime), the following happens at the payment provider:

  • Dodo Payments processes your payment. Their own privacy policy applies to billing, fraud prevention, and tax reporting: dodopayments.com/privacy
  • The information Dodo collects (e.g., email, name, payment method, country) is shared with us only as needed to deliver your license and provide support.

For paid users, we may store:

  • your email address (so we can send you your license key and support replies);
  • a record of the transaction (date, amount, license ID);
  • any support correspondence you've sent us.

We do not sell, share, or monetize this data.

4. Chrome Web Store

The Extension is distributed through the Chrome Web Store. Google may collect aggregate installation and usage statistics. Their privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Local storage

The Extension uses Chrome's local storage to remember your settings (e.g., toggle preferences for Auto-download or Export CSV). This data never leaves your browser and is removed when you uninstall the Extension.

The website deepakthapa.dev does not currently use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. If that changes, this policy will be updated.

6. Your rights

You can:

  • delete all local data the Extension stores by uninstalling it from Chrome;
  • request deletion of any paid-user data we hold by emailing thapadeepak726@gmail.com;
  • request a copy of any data we hold about you.

7. Children

The Extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

We'll update the "Last updated" date when this policy changes. For significant changes, we'll try to notify users via the Chrome Web Store description or this page.

9. Contact

Email thapadeepak726@gmail.com for any privacy questions.
