// FIRST-TIME HELP

Just installed — what now?

Pin the extension to your toolbar

Click the puzzle icon in Chrome's top-right toolbar, find the extension in the list, and click the pin icon next to it. The extension's icon will then sit permanently in your toolbar — one click away whenever you need it.

How do I use it?

Open the platform the extension is built for (e.g. magnific.ai for Magnific Automation), then click the extension's icon in your toolbar. A side panel opens with the queue controls. Paste prompts, configure settings, press start.

Why isn't it doing anything?

A few common causes: (1) you're not on the platform page yet — open the generator before clicking the extension; (2) you're not logged in — these extensions use your own account, so log in first; (3) the platform's UI changed since the last update — email me and I'll patch it within a day.

Are my credentials safe?

The extensions never see, store, or transmit your platform passwords. They operate inside your already-logged-in browser session — the same way any user click on the page would. No login info is sent anywhere, ever.

Found a bug or have a feature request?

Email thapadeepak726@gmail.com. I read every message and most replies go out within 24 hours on weekdays. Bug reports get priority — I patch and ship fast.

When do you charge for these?

Right now everything is free for early users. Once a tool has enough users that I'm confident it's battle-tested, the price flips to $15 lifetime for new customers. If you installed during the free launch, you're grandfathered in at free forever.
