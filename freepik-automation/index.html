// HOW IT WORKS

Three steps. That's the whole product.

STEP 1

Install & open Freepik.

Add the extension to Chrome, log into your Freepik account like normal, and open the video generator page.

STEP 2

Paste your prompt list.

One prompt per line. The extension reads the list and configures each run with your chosen duration, aspect ratio, and style.

STEP 3

Press start. Walk away.

The queue runs in order, waits for each render to finish, and starts the next one — even if you switch tabs. Come back to a finished library.

Is Freepik Automation made by Freepik?

No. Freepik Automation is an independent third-party Chrome extension, not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Freepik Company. It's a browser tool that automates actions you'd otherwise do manually on freepik.com using your own account.

Do I need a Freepik account?

Yes — you use your own Freepik account and your own credits. The extension doesn't include Freepik access; it just automates how you interact with the site once you're logged in.

Is this one-time or a subscription?

One-time payment. $29 gets you a lifetime license for one user, with all future updates included. No subscription, no auto-renewal, no hidden fees.

What if Freepik changes their UI and the extension breaks?

When Freepik rolls out meaningful UI changes, the extension gets patched and pushed to the Chrome Web Store. Updates are free for the lifetime of your license.

What's the refund policy?

7-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If it doesn't work for you, email thapadeepak726@gmail.com within 7 days of purchase and I'll refund via Paddle.

Is my Freepik password safe?

The extension never sees, stores, or transmits your Freepik password. It operates inside your already-logged-in browser session, the same way any user action on the page would. No credentials are sent anywhere.
